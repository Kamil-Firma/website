RELEASING 2026

Hopping Out is a tile-based puzzle game where you hop 2 tiles at a time.
Play as Frog and use your tongue to move boxes, press buttons to create lily pads, all to catch many flies!

PLAY DEMO ON STEAM

Solve over 180 puzzle levels across 7 worlds to help Frog move out of the busy swamp and live a calm life in the city!

Features:

▪ Hop two tiles, or bump into solid walls to stop yourself mid-jump.
▪ Play around with many elements such as deadly bees, bouncy mushrooms, or fly-luring skunks.
▪ Test your puzzle-solving in difficult bonus levels.
▪ Explore a level select map with integrated puzzles and split paths.
▪ Collect and mix-and-match cosmetics in the wardrobe.
